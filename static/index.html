The Model

  • DatasetNLTK twitter_samples — 10,000 labeled tweets
  • Vocabulary~4,400 words at threshold = 2
  • OrdersUnigrams through 6-grams
  • SmoothingAdd-1 (Laplace), k = 1
  • PerplexityComputed in log-space

Sentiment Hybrid

  • BaselinePer-class bigram models, ~93% accuracy
  • NegationPang & Lee 2002 scope marking
  • LexiconHu & Liu 2004 opinion lexicon
  • OverrideOOD + low-confidence detection
